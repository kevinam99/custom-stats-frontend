COVID-19
Daily Custom Statistics

We send daily COVID-19 statistics directly to your email. Please add your email id and choose the regions and statistics you are interested in. We send one email a day. We use the data curated by Johns Hopkins Hospital. (https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19).

This is purely for the purpose of disseminating verified information. We will never share your data with anyone.
